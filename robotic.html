Robotic

Ontario’s first after-school robotics program offers 2 semester based courses yearly. Starts at the beginning of the school semester till the end. The most demanding features of our Robotic program is the robotic project allowing you to work on state of the art technology. The student keeps everything that they make with our tools and resources, such as oscilloscopes, multimeter, power supplies, soldering stations, and access to 3-D printing. Laptop and a signed waiver will be required for safety purposes. The program cost begins at $300 per month for a 5 months period. Pre-register first.

Fall Program

The term project for this course is a legged autonomous robot. This course heavily focuses on the mechanical design, and leaves room for electrical and software beginners. Check out the curriculum here, and to inquire, contact us here.

Spring Program

This course builds on top of the Fall program. It is recommended to complete the Fall program first. The term project for this course is a piano playing robot. The piano playing robot is a demanding robot that requires an in-depth knowledge of integrating software, electrical and mechanical design. The curriculum is TBD, but for inquiries, contact us here.

Hack Challenge

We would like to hold an yearly hack challenge independent from the semester project. This hack challenge is open to all students and encourages application of what has been learnt outside of the classroom into everyday use. The student’s hack should be aimed at solving a real life problem with the help of a robotic solution. The project must include at least 2 of the 3 areas of Software, Mechanical and Electrical. Our tutors will be the judges and bring in a hack of their own to inspire the students.

The following awards will be presented:
Best Overall
Best Creativity
Best Technical
Best Quality
