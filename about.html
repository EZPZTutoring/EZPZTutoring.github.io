About

Getting Started

We offer two separate types of programs, Academic and Robotic. First decide which one suits your need the most by visiting the pages on the website and then inquire us. We will then require an interview to evaluate the level of competency. There is the option to either further improve the strengths or to work on the areas of improvement.

Our programs are not self-learning and will follow the Tutorial structure in Universities. After the 1hr lesson, there will be a 2hr lab session for the Robotic program or a 1hr homework session for the Academic program, where we will be there for resource and guidance. Please refer to the respective pages of the website for more information on each type of program before inquiry. Classes that are offered are subject to availability. Students out of our range will be subject to additional costs to cover transportation.
